What it does

Everything your samples need.

Six core capabilities, built around one goal: get to the right sound faster.

Intelligent Library

SQLite FTS5 index, drag-folder import, hot-remount aware, #tags, star ratings, missing-file relink, saved-search Finder tabs.

signalsmith DSP

Tape, Stretch, and Pitch modes via signalsmith-stretch. True-peak normalisation, reverse, loop region, clip indicator, gain 0 to +24 dB.

Galaxy View

Vertex-cached 2-D scatter of one-shots over MFCC space. Pan / zoom / click-to-audition / drag-to-DAW. Shift-drag lasso for multi-select.

20-Voice Audition

Polyphonic pool with 4096-frame head-cache LRU, steal-oldest fades, background reader reaping, and atomic-pointer-swap transport hand-off.

RX-Style Waveform

Metal compute shader. Filled envelope at overview, signed sample-line at deep zoom, alpha-blended across the transition. RMS-normalised minimap.

Coming soon: AI Variations

Planned for a future update, not in the current release: generate fresh variations of any sample locally on your Mac — new textures, fills, and one-shots without ever leaving your library.
