Version 1.0 — Initial public release

Latest 2026-06-10

What’s in 1.0

  • Local-first library: drag folders in, full-text + tag search, star ratings, saved-search tabs, crates and export baskets.
  • Automatic analysis — accurate BPM and musical key detection, loop vs. one-shot classification, and sound categorisation.
  • Instant 20-voice polyphonic audition with Tape, Stretch, and Pitch playback modes, loop regions, reverse, and per-clip gain.
  • RX-style waveform with a normalised minimap, editable transient markers, and Pro Tools-style clip gain and layered timebase rulers.
  • Galaxy view, Find Similar, Reference Match, and Tempo / Key Match for discovering the right sound fast.
  • Stacks (Shuffler) for layering up to 16 sounds into a phase-locked looping mix.
  • Drag-to-DAW export for moving samples into your session.
  • Library Doctor for finding duplicates, missing files, and other library issues.

Planned for a future update (not in 1.0)

  • Create Variation — generate fresh variations of any sample locally on your Mac.
  • Pro Tools bridge: Spot Raw and Spot Slices straight to a selected or new track.
  • Batch export and Pack Builder power-tools.
