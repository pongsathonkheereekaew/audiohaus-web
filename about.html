The developer

Pongsathon Kheereekaew

The LT · Easby Studios

I make music as The LT and build software as Easby Studios. Auric started as a frustration: every sample manager I tried was either too simple, too slow, or designed for a workflow that wasn’t mine.

So I built one from scratch. C++20 and JUCE 8 on macOS, SQLite FTS5 for instant search across 500k+ files, signalsmith-stretch DSP for real-time time-stretching and pitch-shifting, and an in-house similarity engine for finding related sounds.

Auric is the tool I wanted to exist. I use it every day. That’s why it keeps getting better.
